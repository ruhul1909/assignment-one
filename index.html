About Us

Our goal since the beginning has been to revolutionize green cooking. For us, green food is not about everything being vegetarian, but we want to make it easier, more fun and tastier to eat greener food. We want everyone to know how good and easy it is to use our seeds, beans, lentils and vegetables in daily cooking.

Our Recipies

For several years, we at GoGreen have worked with Lantmännen to grow lentils in Sweden. Our yellow lentils of the variety Flora thrive in more northern latitudes and are grown on farms in southern Sweden. The lens has a darker casing with a bright yellow core and solid shape that fits well in everything, from salads to stews.

Salad Mix

You can use GoGreen Salad Mix as it is, as a snack or lightly roasted in your salad. Feel free to also try chopping the mixture and mixing into the muesli in the morning.

Gröna Linser

GoGreen Green lentils are good in salads, stews, soups and gratins. The lentils do not need to be soaked, but they get bigger and boil faster with 1-2 hours of soaking. Green Lentils can also be germinated!
